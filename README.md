# Waylena

Networking made simple — log conversations, follow up, land opportunities.

Live site: https://waylena.com

## Overview

Waylena is a mobile-first app designed for professionals who want to be more intentional about networking — job seekers, career explorers, and anyone who wants to stay on top of the connections that matter.

The project started as a personal frustration: great conversations fade, follow-ups slip, and valuable connections get lost not because of bad intentions — but bad systems. Waylena is the dedicated space to fix that.

Built with Expo/React Native, Supabase, and PostHog. The marketing site was prototyped with Lovable and deployed on Vercel. The email waitlist runs through Kit with double opt-in to qualify signups.

The core question:

Can a simple, structured logging habit meaningfully improve how professionals build and maintain relationships over time?

## The Problem

Most professionals don't have a networking problem. They have a follow-up problem.

You had a great coffee chat. A mentor gave real advice. Someone offered to make an intro — and then life got in the way. The details faded, the follow-up never got sent, and the connection slipped through the cracks.

Generic contact apps weren't built for this. Waylena is.

## What I Built

Waylena allows users to:

- Log conversations with context (advice received, commitments made, topics discussed)
- Tag connections by category (Career Pivot, Job Lead, etc.)
- Set follow-up reminders so nothing falls through
- Track top connections and build intentional networking habits over time
- Unlock AI-powered narrative synthesis and smart follow-up suggestions (Premium, coming soon)

## What I Learned

- There's a gap between generic CRM tools and the lightweight, relationship-first logging experience professionals actually need.
- Structured inputs — even simple ones — dramatically improve follow-up quality and consistency.
- Shipping a working beta fast is the right way to pressure-test an idea before over-engineering it - but production-ready mobile development requires more rigor than a web prototype.
- The Free tier validates the core habit loop; Premium unlocks the AI layer once the behavior is established.

## Why This Project Matters

Waylena reflects my belief that AI's biggest value isn't replacing human judgment — it's removing the friction that causes people to avoid doing the things they know they should do.

As a marketing leader who thinks about product-led growth, I wanted to explore what a minimum-viable, habit-forming networking tool actually looks like in practice. This is that exploration.
